Hours & Information 

The Western Reserve Historical Society History Center has the following hours:

  • Monday: CLOSED
  • Tuesday-Saturday: 10:00 am - 5:00 pm

Admission costs are as follows:

  • WRHS Members: Free
  • Active Military: Free
  • Adult: $10.00

  • Children (age 3-12): $5.00
