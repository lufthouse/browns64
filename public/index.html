Vision & Mission 

This exhibit showcases the Cleveland Browns Football Team and acts as a commemoration to the 50th anniversary of the 1964 championship season. This exhibit also celebrates the enduring story of the Cleveland Browns, and places the team and the championship win within the context of Cleveland and the US in 1964. The items in the exhibit have been collected to help share the excitement from the 1964 season with younger generations as we move into the 2014 season.

By getting involved with the Western Reserve Historical Society, you will not only support this exhibit, but many more in the future. Your membership fees will help by:

  • Maintaining the artifacts
  • Allowing for new purchases
  • Helping renovate the space
